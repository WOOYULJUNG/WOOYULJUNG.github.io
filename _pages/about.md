I am an MS student at Seoul National University advised by [Prof. Byoung-Tak Zhang](https://bi.snu.ac.kr/~btzhang/), currently a visiting research student at the University of Toronto and a researcher at LG Electronics' Toronto AI Lab. My research is at the intersection of neural dynamics and machine learning. I study associative memory and attractor dynamics through Hopfield networks, and explore how recurrent neural dynamics can enable efficient learning for control and robotic manipulation.

**Research Interests**

- **Neural Dynamics & Associative Memory**: attractor dynamics, Hopfield and modern Hopfield models, dynamical systems analysis of RNNs and reservoir computing, energy-based perspectives on neural computation and representation geometry
- **Neural Dynamics for Control**: dynamical systems models for sequential decision-making and control, recurrent dynamics for working memory, motor planning, and robotic manipulation
